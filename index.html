about us

The SSR Group or SSR Infra Buildtech Pvt. Ltd is an infrastructure development company with a number of prestigious projects in its profile, spread across Delhi and NCR. The Group has experience of more than 20 years in the field of industrial colonization. Apart from real estate, it has years of expertise in investment and mortgage consultancy. The promoters have also been into Non-banking financing for over 8 years which is registered with Reserve Bank of India.
